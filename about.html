About Me

I am a Computer Graphics programmer from Boulder Colorado. I graduated from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo in 2026, with a bachelors of science from the Liberal Arts and Engineering Studies program.

Education: What is LAES?

The Liberal Arts and Engineering Studies program at Calpoly is a multidiciplinary major that combines an Engineering Concentration with a Liberal Arts one with project based courses which combine the skills and diciplines from each.

My concentrations were in Computer Graphics and Visual Art with a focus in animation systems. The computer graphics work was in real time enviroments, as well as post processing. The Visual Arts focus was primarily in 2D and 3D animation, 3D Modeling, Raster and Vector based graphic design, and virtual enviroment production.

Additional general Computer Science coursework satisfied the requirements for an additional Computer Science Minor.
